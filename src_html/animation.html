PN-0

3D AI_BASED

This is an experimental animation that deeply integrates AI-generated technology with human artistic aesthetics. The work boldly deconstructs the classic fable, transplanting Pinocchio's core narrative of "seeking father and redemption" into an absurd yet grand extraterrestrial civilization.

The workflow first employs generative AI to construct this new narrative logic and script details, followed by using AI to generate preliminary storyboards. On the basis of preserving the original tension of the AI-generated content, meticulous manual artistic refinement and aesthetic direction were applied.

Finally, AI models were once again utilized for visual rendering and motion generation. This endeavor represents a redefinition of a classic character in the era of digital life.
