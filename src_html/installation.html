Tangram.me

3d Installation

Tangram.me is a modular mirror system that reinterprets the traditional Tangram puzzle. Composed of eleven geometric pieces, it invites users to reconfigure their own reflection.

The project transforms a functional object into an interactive art piece, reflecting infinite shapes and personal perspectives through the simple yet profound logic of geometric reorganization.

Rinnovare il Quotidiano

RECYCLE

Renewing the Everyday is an innovative design project that breathes new life into discarded materials, transforming them into functional and sustainable art.

Our signature bag is crafted from repurposed bicycle inner tubes—a durable material often destined for landfills. By blending minimalist aesthetics with meticulous craftsmanship, we create pieces that balance durability, style, and utility.

Each bag serves as a symbol of environmental consciousness, fostering an emotional connection between the user and the unique story behind the recycled product.

Bilancia del tempo

REDESIGN

"Equilibrium of Time" merges utility with art, inspired by the ancient Chinese steelyard balance as a symbol of precision and cultural weight.

Crafted from natural wood, the piece features three rotating discs representing tens, units, and months, allowing for manual time adjustment along a central bar displaying the days of the week.

Its minimalist design strikes a perfect balance between ancient tradition and modern aesthetics, turning the act of tracking time into a tactile, ritualistic experience.
