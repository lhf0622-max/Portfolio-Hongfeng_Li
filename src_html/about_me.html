About me

My name is Hongfeng Li. I am a Chinese designer aspiring to become an interdisciplinary designer.

I previously studied Mechatronic Engineering in China and am currently enrolled in the Design and Communication at Politecnico di Torino. With a strong interest in computer graphics as a beginner practitioner, I aim to further explore the possibilities of human–computer interaction by bridging engineering, design, and digital technologies.
