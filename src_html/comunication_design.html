MobiMind

GRAPHIC COMUNICATION

MobiMind is a mobile learning and communication design project that explores how knowledge can be experienced through movement and direct interaction with physical space.

By combining field-based education, digital tools, and location-aware content, the project transforms learning into an active, situated process rather than a passive consumption of information.

Designed as a modular system, MobiMind guides users through real environments while integrating theoretical perspectives on language and communication to inform both its structure and interaction.
